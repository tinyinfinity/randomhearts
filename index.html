Frequently Asked Questions

What is Retro Tweets?
Retro Tweets is a service that allows you to send personalized postcards to your friends or influential individuals (e.g. governors, celebrities, athletes, and CEOs). It’s a unique way to connect.
How does the service work?
Compose your message, select or enter the recipient’s name, and we’ll autopopulate the address if available. After payment, your postcard will be automatically printed and mailed.
Who can I send postcards to?
You can send postcards to anybody you want. We have an address book that includes many notable figures or you can manually enter details.
How is my payment information handled?
We use Stripe for secure payment processing. Your details are encrypted and never stored on our servers.
Can I send postcards to individuals not listed?
Yes! You can manually enter the recipient’s name and address for anyone.
